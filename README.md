An interactive dashboard visualizing **259 publications** from the SCAPIS study (2015–2026), sourced from [PubMed](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/) and the [official SCAPIS publications page](https://www.scapis.org/publications/).

### Features

- KPI cards — total publications, journals, authors, team size, peak year
- Yearly output trend — bar chart with partial-year indicator
- Research topic trends — line chart across cardiovascular, respiratory, imaging, metabolic, biomarkers, risk factors, mental health
- Topic distribution — doughnut breakdown of research focus areas
- Top journals and first authors — horizontal bar charts
- Searchable, sortable, paginated publication table with direct DOI/PubMed links
- Year, topic, and journal filters
